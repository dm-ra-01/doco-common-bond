# About Common Bond

_The human story behind the platform._

---

Common Bond is a Melbourne-based healthcare technology company building
**Receptor** — a preference-driven workforce allocation platform for Australian
hospital medical staff.

We are founded by a single doctor-engineer who has spent equal time on hospital
wards and in server rooms, and who created Receptor because he personally lived
the frustration of opaque, unfair rotation allocation processes.

This section tells that story — structured, honest, and occasionally candid.
