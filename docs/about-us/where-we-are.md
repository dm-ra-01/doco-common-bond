# Where We Are

**Pre-revenue. Actively developing. Well-engineered. Building the right
relationships.**

---

## The Honest Current State

Common Bond is at the stage that most successful enterprise software companies
have to survive: _the gap between a working product and the first paying
customer_. The product is real, the problem is validated, and the market timing
is right. The work right now is converting pilot engagement into signed
agreement and signed agreement into recurring revenue.

This section is intentionally candid. The [Our Aims](./our-aims) page covers
where we are going; this page covers where we actually are.

### What We Are Not Yet

- **Not yet revenue-generating.** We anticipate our first paid engagement in
  conjunction with the FY2026 Monash Health pilot.
- **Not yet ISO 27001 certified.** We are operating to the standard; formal
  certification audit is a FY2027 milestone.
- **Not yet a multi-person team.** The platform is built and maintained by Ryan,
  operating across all functional seats. This is intentional at our stage; we
  are recruiting advisors, not generalists.

## FY2026 Q1 Focus

Right now, the business is focused on three things:

1. **Finalise the Monash Health pilot agreement.** Convert the active engagement
   into a formal pilot SLA, including scope, timeline, success metrics, and
   agreed commercial terms post-pilot.

2. **Complete Allocator production hardening.** The matching engine is
   functionally complete; we are stress-testing against full-scale allocation
   scenarios and refining the constraint weighting model.

3. **Build the PMCV relationship.** Our work at Monash Health has piqued PMCV's
   interest. We are in relationship-building mode — understanding their
   priorities and positioning Receptor as the allocation standard they should
   endorse.

## Our Operational Context

### The Doctor-Developer Reality

Ryan works rostered clinical shifts as an ICU Registrar at Monash Health
alongside building Receptor. This is not a transitional arrangement — it is a
core part of the product philosophy. Receptor is built by someone who is
actively experiencing the problem it solves.

The practical consequence is that development velocity is non-linear: clinical
blocks produce shipping cycles, not sprints. This is an accepted reality, not a
constraint to be apologised for.

### StartSpace

Common Bond is part of the **StartSpace** community — an open, inclusive network
of early-stage founders powered by the State Library Victoria. StartSpace
provides access to free coworking, peer support, and connection to the Melbourne
founder ecosystem. For a solo-founder at this stage, community infrastructure
matters.
