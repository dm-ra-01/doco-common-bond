## 3. High-Fidelity Design Standards

Receptor is the "face" of the medical technology suite. Our design process prioritizes high-fidelity, professional aesthetics to build institutional trust.

- **Human-First UI:** Focus on reducing "Matching Anxiety" for doctors.
- **Enterprise-Grade Styling:** Using professional typography (Outfit) and a refined color palette.
- **Micro-Interactions:** Smooth transitions that make complex data sets feel approachable.
