# Vision

**Standardising Fairness in Medical Workforce Allocation.**

## The Problem

Hospital medical rosters are built on a foundation of "rotations"â€”fixed blocks of clinical experience that rotate throughout the year. The process of assigning hundreds (and for some services, thousands) of doctors to these blocks is currently:

*   **Inefficient:** Manual, spreadsheet-based processes consume thousands of administrative hours every season. Workforce teams are bogged down in logic that should be automated.
*   **Opaque:** Doctors don't understand *why* they received certain rotations. This lack of transparency leads to perceived bias, dissatisfaction, and ultimately, attrition.
*   **High-Risk:** Errors in manual logic lead to accreditation failures for training programs and critical workforce gaps on the front line.

---

## The Solution

Receptor provides the **intelligent matching engine** that replaces manual placement with an auditable, preference-driven algorithm. 

We don't just build a roster; we architect the optimal workforce distribution for the entire clinical year. By resolving complex constraints (site parity, training requirements, leave cover) against doctor preferences, we deliver a "Win-Win" for both the administration and the clinician.
