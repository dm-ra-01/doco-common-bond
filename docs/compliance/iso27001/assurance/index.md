# Assurance

Assurance is how Common Bond verifies the ISMS is working. It is the "check"
component of the Plan–Do–Check–Act cycle. This section contains the procedures
and records for internal audit, non-conformity management, and corrective
actions. ISO 27001:2022 Clauses 9 (Performance Evaluation) and 10 (Improvement).

An internal audit programme is in place, with the first audit scheduled for
**2026-04-15** — two weeks before the target Stage 1 readiness date of
2026-04-30.
