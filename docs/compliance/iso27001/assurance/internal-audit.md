# Internal Audit Procedure

## 1. Purpose
To verify that the ISMS conforms to its own requirements and ISO 27001:2022 standards.

## 2. Schedule
Internal audits are conducted **annually** or before significant external audits.
- **Scope:** Review of Policies, Risk Assessment, and Technical Controls.

## 3. Process
1.  **Plan:** Define scope and select an auditor (independent of the work being audited, e.g., external consultant or cross-functional peer).
2.  **Conduct:** Interview staff, review evidence (logs, configurations), and observe processes.
3.  **Report:** Document findings categorized as:
    - **Conformity:** Innovation/Good Practice.
    - **Non-Conformity (Major/Minor):** Failure to meet a requirement.
    - **Observation:** Opportunity for improvement.

## 4. Reporting
Audit reports are presented to Management and tracked in the **Non-Conformity Log**.
