# Governance

Governance establishes the strategic foundation of Common Bond's ISMS — defining
**why** we invest in information security, **who** is accountable, and the
**boundaries** of what the ISMS covers. ISO 27001:2022 Clauses 4 (Context), 5
(Leadership), and 6 (Planning).

At Common Bond's current stage, all top management and ISM responsibilities are
held by a single named individual: **Ryan Ammendolea, Founder & CEO**. This
dual-role is formally acknowledged and documented.
