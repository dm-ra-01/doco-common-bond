# ISMS Objectives

## 1. Introduction
These objectives align our Information Security Management System (ISMS) with our business goals as a pre-revenue startup. They focus on building trust, ensuring resilience, and maintaining agility.

## 2. Security Objectives

### Objective 1: Build Customer Trust
**Goal:** Demonstrate a robust security posture to unblock enterprise sales.
- **Metric:** Achieve ISO 27001 readiness (Stage 1 audit capable) by [Target Date].
- **Target:** 0 blockers in customer vendor risk assessments related to basic security hygiene.

### Objective 2: Secure the Platform (Product Security)
**Goal:** Prevent data breaches in the Receptor platform.
- **Metric:** Critical vulnerabilities in production.
- **Target:** 0 critical vulnerabilities unresolved for > 48 hours.
- **Initiative:** Automated vulnerability scanning (GitHub Dependabot, Supabase security headers) enabled on all repos.

### Objective 3: Ensure Availability
**Goal:** Maximize uptime for customer operations.
- **Metric:** Platform availability uptime.
- **Target:** 99.9% uptime during business hours.
- **Initiative:** Redundant infrastructure on Supabase and Cloudflare failover configurations.

### Objective 4: Incident Readiness
**Goal:** Ability to detect and respond to security incidents.
- **Metric:** Time to detect and acknowledge security alerts.
- **Target:** < 4 hours for high-severity alerts.
- **Initiative:** Centralized logging and alerting via Supabase and external monitoring tools.

## 3. Monitoring and Measurement
These objectives are reviewed **annually** during the Management Review meeting or sooner if significant changes tailored to the business occur (e.g., major pivot, funding round).
