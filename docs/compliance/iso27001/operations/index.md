# Operations

The Operations section contains Common Bond's operational evidence — the
records, registers, and procedures that demonstrate the ISMS is not just
described but actually running. These documents answer the auditor's question:
_"Do you actually do what your policies say?"_ ISO 27001:2022 Clauses 7–10 and
Annex A.

## Documents in This Section

| Document                                              | ISO Annex A     | Purpose                                                                                                               |
| ----------------------------------------------------- | --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [Statement of Applicability (SoA)](./soa)             | All 93 controls | Definitive record of which Annex A controls apply, justification, and implementation status                           |
| [Asset Register](./asset-register)                    | 5.9             | 15 information assets across 4 categories (Information, Software, Hardware, People) with owners and recovery priority |
| [Incident Response Plan](./incident-response)         | 5.24–5.28       | Severity taxonomy; 5-phase response process; Privacy Act NDB 72-hour notification; OAIC obligations                   |
| [Business Continuity Plan](./business-continuity)     | 5.29, 5.30      | RTO 4 hours / RPO 12 hours; Supabase PITR as primary recovery mechanism; three disruption scenarios                   |
| [Supplier Security Register](./supplier-register)     | 5.19–5.22       | 6 critical suppliers; DPA status; APP 8 compliance actions per supplier; Sentry PHI-scrubbing policy                  |
| [Training & Competency Policy](./training-competency) | 6.3             | Required training programme; onboarding checklist structure; competency requirements                                  |
| [Training Records](./training-records)                | 6.3             | Live evidence record: competency matrix for all staff; onboarding completion status                                   |
