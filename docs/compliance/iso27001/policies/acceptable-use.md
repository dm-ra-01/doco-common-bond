# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

## 1. Purpose
This policy outlines the acceptable use of Common Bond's information assets and systems to protect them from compromise.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties accessing Common Bond systems.

## 3. Principles
1.  **Professional Use:** Systems are provided for business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted if it does not interfere with work or pose a security risk.
2.  **No Illegal Activity:** Prohibited use includes copyright infringement, harassment, or accessing illegal content.
3.  **Asset Protection:** Users must take reasonable care to protect devices (e.g., locking screens, not leaving devices unattended in public).

## 4. Specific Requirements

### 4.1 Passwords & Authentication
- Use strong, unique passwords (generated by a Password Manager).
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** must be enabled on all accounts (GitHub, Google Workspace, Supabase, Cloudflare).
- Do not share passwords.

### 4.2 Devices
- Install all critical OS and browser updates within 7 days.
- Ensure full-disk encryption (FileVault/BitLocker) is enabled.
- Use a screen lock with a short timeout (e.g., 5 minutes).

### 4.3 Data Handling
- Do not store sensitive customer data on local devices used for development unless necessary and encrypted.
- Use approved cloud storage (Google Drive, GitHub) for document sharing.

### 4.4 Reporting
- Report lost or stolen devices immediately to the CTO.
- Report suspected phishing attempts or suspicious activity.
