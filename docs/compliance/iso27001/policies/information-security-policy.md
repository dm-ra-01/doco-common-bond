# Information Security Policy

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to protect the information assets of Common Bond (Receptor Platform) from all internal and external threats, ensuring business continuity and minimizing damage.

## 2. Policy Statement
Common Bond is committed to:
- Establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an Information Security Management System (ISMS) in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.
- Satisfying applicable information security requirements, including legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.
- Setting and reviewing information security objectives.
- ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

## 3. Core Principles
1.  **Risk-Based Approach:** Security controls are selected based on a formal risk assessment.
2.  **Least Privilege:** Access to information and systems is granted on a need-to-know basis.
3.  **Defense in Depth:** Multiple layers of security controls are implemented.
4.  **Continuous Improvement:** The ISMS is regularly reviewed and improved.

## 4. Responsibilities
- **CEO:** Approves this policy and ensures resources are available.
- **CTO/ISM:** Ensures policy implementation and compliance monitoring.
- **All Staff:** Must comply with this policy and report any security incidents.

## 5. Non-Compliance
Breaches of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract.
