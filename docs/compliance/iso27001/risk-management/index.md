# Risk Management

Risk management is the analytical core of the ISMS. Common Bond uses a
qualitative likelihood × impact scoring approach to identify information
security risks, assess their severity, and select appropriate treatment
strategies. ISO 27001:2022 Clauses 6.1 (Risk Assessment) and 6.2 (Risk
Treatment).

Risks are scored on a 3×3 matrix. The risk acceptance threshold and approval
authority at each risk level are defined in the Methodology. All risk acceptance
decisions require CEO approval.
