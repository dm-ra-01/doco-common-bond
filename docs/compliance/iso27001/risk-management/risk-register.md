# Risk Register

| Version | Effective Date | Approved By          | Next Review |
| ------- | -------------- | -------------------- | ----------- |
| 1.0     | ⚠️ Confirm     | Ryan Ammendolea, CEO | ⚠️ Confirm  |

This is the authoritative Risk Register for Common Bond's Information Security
Management System (ISMS). It is maintained as a live record and reviewed
annually or upon significant organisational or threat changes.

## Risk Register

| ID    | Description                                           | Threat                                                                                                                                                                                                              | Impact                                                                                                | Risk Level | Risk Owner            | Treatment Strategy                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | Status      |
| :---- | :---------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :--------- | :-------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :---------- |
| R-001 | **Laptop Loss/Theft**                                 | Data leak via stolen device                                                                                                                                                                                         | Confidentiality breach                                                                                | Medium     | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Full-disk encryption (FileVault) & strong passwords enforced                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | Implemented |
| R-002 | **Phishing Attack**                                   | Credential theft leading to unauthorised access                                                                                                                                                                     | Unauthorised access to systems and data                                                               | High       | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** MFA enforced on all accounts + security awareness training                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | Implemented |
| R-003 | **Cloud Misconfiguration**                            | Database or storage resource inadvertently exposed                                                                                                                                                                  | Data breach                                                                                           | High       | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Infrastructure-as-code reviews; Supabase RLS policies enforced at data layer                                                                                                                                                                                                  | Implemented |
| R-004 | **Dependency Vulnerabilities**                        | Exploitation of known vulnerabilities in third-party libraries                                                                                                                                                      | System compromise or data exposure                                                                    | Medium     | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Dependabot alerts enabled on all repositories; regular dependency updates                                                                                                                                                                                                     | Ongoing     |
| R-005 | **Insider Threat**                                    | Intentional or accidental data exfiltration by staff                                                                                                                                                                | Data breach, reputational damage                                                                      | Medium     | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Least-privilege access controls; background checks for all personnel                                                                                                                                                                                                          | Ongoing     |
| R-006 | **Key Person Dependency**                             | Loss or unavailability of the Founder/CEO (sole technical and business decision-maker)                                                                                                                              | Inability to operate or make critical decisions; ISMS continuity risk                                 | High       | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Document all critical processes; establish emergency contact escalation path. **Accept** (residual) pending growth of team.                                                                                                                                                   | Ongoing     |
| R-007 | **Regulatory Breach / Privacy Act Non-Compliance**    | Failure to meet Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) or notify affected individuals within required timeframe following a data breach                                                                               | Regulatory penalties, reputational damage, customer trust loss                                        | High       | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Incident Response Plan in place; data classification scheme implemented; breach notification procedures documented                                                                                                                                                            | Planned     |
| R-008 | **Third-Party / Supplier Failure**                    | Critical supplier (Supabase, Cloudflare, GitHub) experiences an outage, data breach, or ceases to operate                                                                                                           | Service unavailability, data loss, or loss of source code access                                      | Medium     | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Supabase PITR configured (RPO 12 hours); Cloudflare failover; GitHub data export taken regularly. **Transfer** (partial) via supplier SLAs                                                                                                                                    | Ongoing     |
| R-009 | **Lack of Incident Response Capability**              | Security incident occurs without a documented, tested response process                                                                                                                                              | Extended breach duration, failure to meet Privacy Act notification obligations, regulatory exposure   | High       | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Incident Response Plan being drafted (see operations/incident-response.md). Interim: alert@commonbond.au monitored by CEO                                                                                                                                                     | Planned     |
| R-010 | **Business Continuity Failure**                       | Extended unavailability of the platform due to infrastructure failure, disaster, or key person incapacity                                                                                                           | Customer operations disrupted; contractual and reputational consequences                              | Medium     | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Business Continuity Plan being drafted. Supabase PITR satisfies RPO. RTO target: 4 hours documented                                                                                                                                                                           | Planned     |
| R-011 | **Customer Data Retention Obligation Non-Compliance** | Hospital customers may impose statutory retention requirements (up to 7 years under applicable state health records legislation), creating tension with Common Bond's ability to delete data on Privacy Act request | Regulatory breach; contractual liability; inability to comply with subject access or erasure requests | High       | Ryan Ammendolea (CEO) | **Reduce:** Develop a formal data retention and deletion schedule documenting per-customer retention obligations; obtain customer consent terms that clarify retention periods; seek legal advice on intersection of Privacy Act erasure obligations and statutory retention requirements | Planned     |
