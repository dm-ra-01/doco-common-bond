:::note[Risk Acceptance Criteria]

**Risk acceptance threshold:** Risks rated **High** require an active treatment
plan and must not be accepted without documented CEO approval. Risks rated
**Medium** may be accepted with documented rationale. Risks rated **Low** are
monitored annually.

Risk levels are derived from a qualitative 3×3 matrix (Likelihood × Impact):

- **High:** Likely + High Impact, or Certain + Medium Impact
- **Medium:** Possible + High Impact, or Likely + Medium Impact
- **Low:** All other combinations

:::
