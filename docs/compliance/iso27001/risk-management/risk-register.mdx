## Risk Level Definitions

| Level | Likelihood × Impact | Description |
|:------|:---|:---|
| **Critical** | 4–5 × 4–5 | Existential or regulatory breach risk — immediate treatment required |
| **High** | 3–4 × 3–4 | Significant operational or compliance impact — active treatment plan required |
| **Medium** | 2–3 × 2–3 | Moderate impact — treatment scheduled and monitored |
| **Low** | 1–2 × 1–2 | Minimal impact — accepted or under routine monitoring |

## Treatment Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Ongoing** | Active treatment in progress — evidence link required (ISO 27001 Clause 8.3) |
| **Planned** | Treatment scheduled but not yet commenced |
| **Accepted** | Risk formally accepted by management — residual risk within appetite |
| **Transferred** | Risk transferred to a third party (e.g., insurance, contractual obligation) |
| **Closed** | Risk resolved or no longer applicable |
