## 1. Background

MyJMO Pty Ltd is the sole developer, owner, and holder of all intellectual
property rights in and to the Software. Common Bond Pty Ltd is a company
incorporated under the laws of Australia for the purpose of commercialising and
operating the Software as a managed platform for healthcare workforce
management. The parties wish to record the terms on which MyJMO Pty Ltd grants
Common Bond Pty Ltd an exclusive licence to use and commercialise the Software.

## 3. Grant of Licence

**3.1 Exclusive Licence.** Subject to Common Bond Pty Ltd's compliance with this
Agreement, MyJMO Pty Ltd grants to Common Bond Pty Ltd an **exclusive,
worldwide, royalty-free** (subject to any agreed commercial terms) licence
during the Licence Period to:

(a) access, use, copy, operate, and deploy the Software (in source and object
code form) for any purpose connected with the Licensee's business;

(b) make the Software available to End Users via a SaaS or managed-service
model;

(c) modify, adapt, and create derivative works of the Software;

(d) sublicence use of the Software to End Users on terms no less protective of
the Licensor's Intellectual Property Rights than this Agreement; and

(e) market, promote, and commercialise the Software and the Service.

**3.2 Exclusivity.** During the Licence Period, MyJMO Pty Ltd shall not grant
any licence of the Software to any third party without Common Bond Pty Ltd's
prior written consent.

**3.3 Retained Rights.** MyJMO Pty Ltd retains the right to continue developing,
improving, and maintaining the Software for the benefit of the Licensee.

---

## 4. Intellectual Property

**4.1 Ownership.** Title to and all Intellectual Property Rights in the Software
— including all updates, enhancements, and derivative works — vest exclusively
in and remain with MyJMO Pty Ltd. Nothing in this Agreement transfers any
ownership interest in the Software to Common Bond Pty Ltd or any third party.

**4.2 Acknowledgement.** Common Bond Pty Ltd acknowledges that it acquires no
ownership right, title, or interest in the Software and that its rights are
limited to those expressly granted in clause 3.

**4.3 Improvements.** Any improvements or derivative works created by or for
Common Bond Pty Ltd using the Software shall automatically vest in MyJMO Pty Ltd
upon creation, and Common Bond Pty Ltd hereby assigns (and agrees to do all
things necessary to effect that assignment) all Intellectual Property Rights in
such improvements to MyJMO Pty Ltd. The licence in clause 3 extends to all such
improvements.

**4.4 No Source Code Transfer.** Notwithstanding the grant under clause 3.1(a),
no source code or ownership interest in the Software is transferred by this
Agreement.

## 5. Permitted Use and Restrictions

**5.1 Permitted Use.** Common Bond Pty Ltd may:

(a) host and operate the Software for the purpose of providing a managed SaaS
service to End Users;

(b) modify and adapt the Software for operational, product, or feature
development purposes;

(c) engage subcontractors and employees to assist with the operation and
development of the Software, provided those persons are bound by confidentiality
obligations no less stringent than those in clause 8.

**5.2 Restrictions.** Common Bond Pty Ltd must not:

(a) sublicense, sell, lease, lend, or otherwise transfer the Software or any
portion thereof to any third party except as expressly permitted under clause
3.1(d);

(b) represent to any third party that it owns the Software or hold itself out as
the developer of the Software;

(c) remove or alter any proprietary notices, legends, or copyright markings on
the Software.

## 6. Annual Review and Fee Adjustment

**6.1** Any licence fee or commercial arrangement between the parties is subject
to review annually. MyJMO Pty Ltd may vary the commercial terms effective from
the next anniversary of the Agreement Date upon not less than 30 days' written
notice to Common Bond Pty Ltd.

**6.2** Common Bond Pty Ltd may terminate this Agreement on 30 days' written
notice if it does not accept revised commercial terms; continued use of the
Software after that notice period constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

---

## 7. No Perpetual Rights

Payment of any licence fee or other consideration does not confer any perpetual,
irrevocable, or vested rights in the Software. Continued use of the Software
requires ongoing compliance with all terms of this Agreement and is subject to
termination in accordance with clause 10.

## 8. Confidentiality, Privacy and Data Protection

**8.1** Each party shall keep the other party's Confidential Information
strictly confidential and shall not disclose or use it except as required to
exercise rights or perform obligations under this Agreement.

**8.2** Each party shall use reasonable technical and organisational measures to
protect the other party's Confidential Information from unauthorised disclosure.

**8.3** Each party must comply with the _Privacy Act 1988_ (Cth) and the
Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) in relation to any Personal Information
processed in connection with the Software or the Service.

**8.4** Common Bond Pty Ltd must notify MyJMO Pty Ltd of any eligible data
breach (within the meaning of the _Privacy Act 1988_ (Cth)) relating to data
processed under this Agreement within 48 hours of becoming aware of it.

**9.2** Subject to clause 9.1, MyJMO Pty Ltd provides the Software on an "as is"

non-infringement.

**9.3** Nothing in this Agreement excludes or limits liability for: (a) death or

**9.4** Subject to clauses 9.1–9.3, MyJMO Pty Ltd's aggregate liability for all

## 10. Termination

**10.1 Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement by
written notice with immediate effect if the other party:

(a) materially breaches any term of this Agreement and (where the breach is
capable of remedy) fails to remedy that breach within 30 days of receiving
written notice; or

(b) becomes insolvent, is placed into administration, liquidation, or
receivership, or makes any arrangement with creditors.

**10.2 Termination for Convenience.** Either party may terminate this Agreement
on 60 days' written notice to the other party.

**10.3 Consequences of Termination.** Upon termination or expiry:

(a) the licence granted under clause 3 immediately ceases;

(b) Common Bond Pty Ltd must immediately cease all use of the Software,
including any operation of the Service using the Software; and

(c) within 14 days, Common Bond Pty Ltd must certify in writing that all access
credentials, copies, instances, and deployments of the Software have been
decommissioned, destroyed, or returned, except as required by applicable law.
