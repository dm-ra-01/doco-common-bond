### Hire 1: The Optimization Specialist
**Role:** Full-Stack Engineer / Data Scientist
**GWC:** *Get it:* Optimization algorithms and distributed systems. *Want it:* To solve real-world healthcare friction. *Capacity:* 10+ years modern development (React/Supabase).
**Impact:** Scaling the "Matcher" engine to handle nationwide multi-site networks.

### Hire 2: The Hospital Liaison
**Role:** Customer Success Lead
**GWC:** *Get it:* Hospital politics and Workforce workflows. *Want it:* Better tools for their peers. *Capacity:* Former Medical Workforce Manager or Senior DCT.
**Impact:** Speaking the "language" of our clients to shorten the onboarding cycle.

### Hire 3: The Governance Lead
**Role:** Compliance & Data Privacy Officer
**GWC:** *Get it:* ISO 27001, SOC2, and Australian Privacy Principles. *Want it:* Clinical data integrity. *Capacity:* Experience in health-tech security.
**Impact:** Institutional-grade security assurance for government tenders.

## Advisory Board (The Brain Trust)

Scaling beyond the founder requires recruiting a high-impact advisory board:

1.  **Retired Director of Medical Services (DMS):** To provide "Top-Down" procurement insights and board-level credibility.
2.  **Chief Medical Information Officer (CMIO):** To guide technical integration with EMR systems (Epic/Cerner).
3.  **Workforce Analytics Expert:** To refine our "Predictive Attrition" data models.

## The EOS "Accountability" Approach

We hire specifically for the **Accountability Chart** (see [Accountability Chart](./accountability-chart)). Every hire must demonstrate:
*   **G** (Gets it): Do they deeply understand the role?
*   **W** (Wants it): Do they love our mission to standardise fairness?
*   **C** (Capacity): Do they have the technical/clincial skills to deliver?
