### Core Focus
*   **Purpose:** Standardising fairness in healthcare medical workforce allocation.
*   **Niche:** Intelligent matching engines and preference-driven clinical rotation architecture.

### 10-Year Target
Receptor as the **standard national infrastructure** for all Australian healthcare trainee allocations (Medical, Nursing, Allied Health, and Specialist Colleges).

### Competitive Strengths (The "How We Win")
*   **The Doctor-Developer Advantage:** Bridging the gap between SQL and SOP (Standard Operating Procedure).
*   **Established Relationships:** Proven track record with PMCV, Monash Health, and adaptation to AMC Clinical Experience categories.
*   **Commercial Validation:** Successfully licensed software to competitors, proving tangible asset value.

### Why Now? (Market Timing)
1.  **Retention Crisis:** Fairness in allocation is no longer a luxury; it is a critical retention strategy.
2.  **AMC Framework 2024:** Stricter regulatory requirements demand automated compliance.
3.  **Digitisation Mandate:** State health departments are actively seeking "Digital Health" with proven ROI.
