# Common Bond: Corporate Identity

**Common Bond** is the parent entity, representing the foundational unity and commitment between healthcare innovation and professional excellence.

## Visual Language
Our corporate identity is designed to be calm, professional, and trustworthy.

### Color Palette
We use the **Teal & Slate** palette, which evokes a sense of balanced clinical innovation and structural stability.

| Element | Color | Token |
|:---|-|:-|
| **Primary (Teal)** | `#0d9488` | `brand-600` |
| **Secondary (Slate)** | `#475569` | `neutral-600` |
| **Title Text** | `#1e293b` | `neutral-800` |
| **Subtext / Tagline** | `#64748b` | `neutral-500` |

### Typography
- **Primary Typeface**: **Outfit**
- **Headings**: Outfit Bold (700)
- **Body / Tagline**: Outfit Regular (400)

## Brand Values
- **Foundational Unity**: Strengthening the link between clinicians and hospital systems.
- **Modern Professionalism**: Clean, accessible design that reflects our disciplined approach.
- **Future-Proof**: Built to scale alongside the evolving healthcare landscape.
