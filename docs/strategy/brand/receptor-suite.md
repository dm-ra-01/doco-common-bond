# Receptor Suite: Product Identity

**Receptor** is our flagship software platform, designed for the high-performance needs of medical workforce management. Its identity is built on precision, signaling, and algorithmic excellence.

## Visual Language
The product identity is clinical, sharp, and data-driven.

### Color Palette
We use the **Indigo & Slate** palette, which conveys depth, intelligence, and high-tech precision.

| Element | Color | Token |
|:---|-|:-|
| **Primary (Indigo)** | `#4f46e5` | `brand-600` |
| **Deep (Indigo)** | `#312e81` | `brand-900` |
| **UI Surface** | `#f8fafc` | `neutral-50` |
| **Emphasis Text** | `#1e293b` | `neutral-800` |

### Typography
- **Primary Typeface**: **Inter** (Default Google Font)
- **Headings**: Inter Bold (700)
- **UI Elements**: Inter Medium (500)
