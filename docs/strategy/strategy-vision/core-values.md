**Standardising Fairness in Medical Workforce Allocation.**

## Our Mission

Receptor exists to solve the universal challenge of fair job rotation matching in the healthcare sector. We provide the **intelligent matching engine** that replaces manual placement with an auditable, preference-driven algorithm. 

We don't just build a roster; we architect the optimal workforce distribution for the entire year, ensuring that every doctor receives a "Receipt of Fairness."
