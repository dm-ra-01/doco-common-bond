## Executive Summary

**Receptor is the Intelligence Layer for medical workforce allocation.**

We exist to solve the **Retention Crisis** in Australian public hospitals. By moving from opaque spreadsheets to transparent, algorithmic allocation, we empower Health Services to become employers of choice.

### Core Strategic Themes

*   **Move from Admin to Strategy:** Automate the rote work so Managers can plan careers.
*   **Move from Rostering to Pathways:** Focus on the long-term rotation journey, not just the daily shift.
*   **Move from Black Box to Glass Box:** Build trust through radical transparency.
