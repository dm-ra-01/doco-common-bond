## Critical Appraisal

To leverage our strengths and mitigate threats, Receptor must:
1.  **Lead with Clinical Credibility:** The "Doctor-Developer" angle is our primary shield against legacy giants.
2.  **Productize the Audit:** Move from manual allocation audits to an automated "Health Check" tool to shorten the sales cycle.
3.  **Fill Seats Early:** Move from a "Single Founder" model to an "EOS Accountability" model (see [Accountability Chart](../../operations/eos/accountability-chart)) to demonstrate scalability to institutional buyers.
